Sleepea

Sleepea are premium sleep pods catered to your everyday sleep needs. Sleepea was designed for young adults who have trouble falling asleep, providing features that can enable them to fall asleep easier through music, LED lights and many more features.

Arcadia

Arcadia is a recruitment campaign that destigmatises and uplifts rural healthcare. Our team looked at helping nurses looking to work rurally overcome barriers and defeat the stigmas that they may come across. This was a group project that looked at how to make healthcare more accessible to our fellow New Zealanders.

The Prodigal Son

The Prodigal Son was a Passion Project, designed to create an easier platform for young adults who are visual learners to be able to understand the Bible better. This project explored the medium of scrollytelling and went through a range of experiments to reach the final outcome.
