Jefritz Espino

UX/UI DESIGNER


Kia Ora, I'm Jefritz, a creative designer studying at Media Design School, & based in Auckland.

I am passionate about creating and delivering impactful design that benefits the community. There's something significant when designing products that benefit and solve real world problems that can create an impact for a wide range of communities in the future. Challenges, variety & experimentation are always welcomed because that means growth is being done.

I specialise in UX/UI Design, but I am also skilled in coding, graphic design, and illustrating. However, it is important for me to always seeking new learning opportunities so I can expand my creative skillset.

Some soft skills I have are leadership, teaching, eager to learn and problem solving. These are shown through being a Young Adults leader at church, being selected for the We The Women Girl Boss Hackfest 2025, working as an after-school care teacher for children interested in STEM, and volunteering in the lighting team for church where we face troubleshooting and have to problem solve each time.

My hard skills lie in the Adobe Software, Figma, Lightroom, HTML, CSS, JS, Scratch Coding, AI and Procreate.

connection

Design is about connection, connecting ideas, people and communities. The relationships I have with the people I work with is built on trust, and respect. Alongside this, I ensure that my design connects communities through conversations and understanding.

empathy

When we design, we are designing for people. I am committed to understanding the needs, emotions and challenges of every person that is involved in each project. Ensuring that their perspectives and experiences are reflected and heard through the solutions created.

growth

Design is an ever-evolving subject. Each project is designed with care and purpose, hoping to influence growth, whether it's a website, brand, or campaign. In a personal aspect, I create with the intent to grow my skills by taking on new challenges, coming out of my comfort zone and trying new things.

impact

Impact is IMPORTANT. It evokes emotion, creates memories and leaves people thinking or leaving with a new perspective. I strive to create this experience with each design project. Hoping to leave a memorable experience and impact on every person's mind that comes into contact with the projects.

I would love to collaborate on fun projects, hear about your ideas, and/or catch up over dessert.
